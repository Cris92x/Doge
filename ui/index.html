EssentialDogCare

Care for the one that never stops caring.


EssentialDogCare will help you keep safe the most loyal of friends.


  Nutrition

Finding what the right food to feed your dog can be difficult. EssentialDogCare will help guide you to find the best foods for your dog based on his age, size, and breed, so you can rest assured your pet is never hungry and always healthy.

  Health

There are many hazardous things that can harm our pets. From our trash-bin to our supply closets pets face many dangers. EssentialDogCare provides you with the information you need to know should your dog encounter these medical hazards.

  Recalls

Every year hundreds of dog food brands are recalled because of dangers such as salmonella contamination. EssentialDogCare works to provide you with an up-to-date list of recalled brands so that you never have to risk unnecessary veterinary visits.
