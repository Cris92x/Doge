Avacodo
Avocado leaves, fruit, seeds, and bark may contain a toxic principle know as persin. The most problematic appears to be the Guatemalan avocados that are more commonly found in stores. If your dog eats avocado there will not be serious signs of illness.
Symptoms: Mild stomach upset may occur if your dog eats significant amount of avocado.
Warning: Ingestion of the pit can lead to obstruction in the gastrointestinal tract, which is a serious hazard that requires urgent veterinary care.
Macadamia Nuts
Macadamia nut toxicosis is not fatal in dogs, however it can cause your dog to become very uncomfortable.
Symptoms: Weakness in the rear legs, appear to be in pain, may have tremors and may develop a low fever. These signs should subside over 48 hours.
Warning: If your dog is experiencing more mild symptoms, visit the veterinary care to ease the pain, which includes intravenous fluid therapy and pain control.
