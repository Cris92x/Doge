Vitamins and Minerals


Your dog's diet should consist of vitamins and minerals. These essentials are needed to keep your dog healthy and strong. These ingredients are usually added to dog food recipes as vitamin and mineral supplements.

  • Fat-soluable vitamins are vitamin A, D, E and K. Water-soluable vitamins are B-complex vitamins and vitamin C. Essential minerals are found in common fruits, vegetables, meats, and whole grain ingredients.
  • Natural sources of vitamins include carrots, apples, cranberries,peas, and broccoli. Minerals are necessary in a well-balanced dog food. The ingredient list should mention mineral supplements.
  • Avoid lower-quality ingredients such as tallow or lard.

Fish oils such as herring or salmon and flaxseed and canola oils are sources of omega-3 fatty acids. Pork fat, chicken fat, safflower and sunflower oils, corns, vegetable oils are considered omega-6 fatty acid.
