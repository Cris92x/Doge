Protein


Your dog's diet should consist 20-70% of protein. Protein is very important part of a healthy, balanced diet. Protein help build and repair muscles and other body tissues. It is needed to form skin cells, hair, muscle tissue, and much more. Protein provides energy and keeps immune system strong.

  • Proteins are made up of amino acids, and dog requires 22 amino acids to make necessary proteins. Deficiencies of any of the essential amino acids over time can lead to health issues.
  • A protein source should be the first few ingredients listed on the dog food.
  • Protein is found in meats, eggs, and dairy. Depending on the lifestyle of your dog protein needs may vary. Avoid lower-quaility ingredients such as tallow or lard.

Fish oils such as herring or salmon and flaxseed and canola oils are sources of omega-3 fatty acids. Pork fat, chicken fat, safflower and sunflower oils, corns, vegetable oils are considered omega-6 fatty acid.
